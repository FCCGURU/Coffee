Coffee's History

Coffee was founded in the 15th-17th century in the Yemeni region.

Although popular worldwide, it has a long history tied to food traditions around the Red Sea.

Learn more here.


Fun Facts

  • Coffee is a beverage brewed from roasted, ground beans.

  • Although usually served hot, chilled and iced options are popular picks.

  • Coffee has a stimulating effect on humans, primarily due to its caffeine content.

Recipe

  1. Measure coffee grounds & place in filter

  2. Add water & brew, remember filtered water > tap water

  3. Enjoy!
